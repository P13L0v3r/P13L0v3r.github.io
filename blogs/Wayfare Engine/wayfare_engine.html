The Wayfare Engine

The Wayfare Engine is a method of drawing cards to create events (commonly called encounters in other systems) to interact with. The events provided here are basic and versatile, while remaining inspiring.

The Wayfare Engine is intended to be used in roleplaying games that focus on exploration, discovery, or advancement. They can also be inserted into a system as a way of generating random encounters or used in conjunction with an oracle in solo games.

Events

To determine the kind of event encountered, simply draw a card from a deck (excluding the jokers) and consult the following table, reading from left to right. You can use a whole deck, arrange the cards face down in a grid to create a map for a dungeon or wilderness crawl, or some other creative way of drawing the cards that feels appropriate.

Card Event
Face Black Combat
Red Emergency
Number Clubs Counsel
Spades Calm
Hearts
Diamonds Advent

Here are the brief descriptions of each event. They are left as guidelines for your own elaboration later. If you are so inclined, you can create your own or find supplementary tables or oracles to determine the details for the event. The Wayfare Engine is simply a template for exploration and discovery.

Advent

An Advent is some milestone of discovery. The group gains knowledge about some aspect of the world.

Calm

A Calm is a period where very little happens outside the group. This is an opportunity for players and characters to roleplay between themselves.

Combat

A Combat is some attack by an outside force. Enemies of the group seek to do them harm.

Counsel

A Counsel is a roleplay interaction with a faction outside of the group, usually requiring diplomacy and tact to resolve some issue the outside faction faces.

Emergency

An Emergency is a sudden disaster that the group faces that will result in harm to the group.
